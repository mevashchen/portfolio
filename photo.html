"Photography is one of my favorite ways to capture moments in time and express my personal perspective of life. I started taking photos at around 11 years old using the app 'Hipstamatic' on my iPod. It was a point and shoot film camera emulator that let me shoot photos in a myriad of different ways. It was magic. I remember there was a time when I was using the app to take sepia and black & white photos more than the iPod camera itself. Using this app inevitably led me to discovering both digital and film photography. This is a collection of 35mm film, 120mm film, and digital photos that I have been taking over the course of about 5 years."
